# The Little Book That Still Beats the Market

### Good business at bargain price

高资产回报率 Return on Capital（ROC）

高盈利率 Earning Yield（EY）

### Formula

ROC = EBIT / net fixed assets + working capital

EY= EBIT / EV

EBIT：息税前利润

EV：企业价值
